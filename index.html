Our Process

Why We're Different

We're a small, local team based between Cape Cod and Lowell, MA, not a corporate agency with overhead costs and subscription traps. We build professional websites at prices that make sense for small businesses because we keep things simple, efficient, and honest.

You pay once for the design and build. After that, the website is yours to keep: no ongoing fees to us, no subscriptions to stay live. We'll help you set up hosting with a provider of your choice (we recommend options like Namecheap or Bluehost), and you'll pay them directly, typically around $20-$30/month. This is a standard cost for running any website, and we'll guide you through the entire setup process.

Even with hosting factored in, our approach saves you thousands compared to agency retainers or monthly subscription platforms like Wix and Squarespace. You get a professional website, full ownership, and the freedom to manage it however you want.

You Own It. Fully.

No strings attached. No hidden costs. No recurring subscriptions to keep your site online. Once we build it, it's yours: the files, the design, the content. You can update it yourself, hire someone else, or come back to us. Complete freedom.
